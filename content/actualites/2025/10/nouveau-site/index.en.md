Volley Club Nogentais is delighted to present its brand‑new website! Simple, clear and easy to browse, it is now live to
keep you informed of all club news.

<!--more-->

### A static site, always up‑to‑date

Even though the site is static, we commit to updating it regularly to provide you with:

- **Upcoming matches** of the **FSGT** and **FFVB** teams (soon, we promise!): dates, times and venues, so you never
  miss a game.
- **Results**: follow the performance of your favourite teams.
- **Practical information**: training schedules, contacts, and everything you need to know to join us.

### A tool for players and supporters

Whether you are a licensed member, a parent, or simply a volleyball enthusiast, this site is built for you:

- **Quick access to information**: no more hunting around, everything is centralized.
- **Useful links**: direct access to the registration form (via a partner site) and club resources.

### How to follow us?

- Visit the site: [volley-club-nogent.fr](https://volley-club-nogent.fr)
- Stay connected: follow us on

  [{{< fontawesome class="fa-brands fa-instagram"  font-size="1.5em" >}} Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/volleyclubnogent/)
  for real‑time updates and
  [{{< fontawesome class="fa-brands fa-youtube" font-size="1.5em" >}} Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@volleyclubnogent)
  for match replays.

### A project that grows with you

This website is just the first step. We rely on your feedback to enrich and improve it. Feel free to send us your
suggestions or ideas!

### Thank you all!

A huge thank‑you to everyone who contributed to creating this site, as well as our partners and volunteers. Together,
let’s keep volleyball alive in Nogent!

**See you soon on the courts… and online!** 🏐
