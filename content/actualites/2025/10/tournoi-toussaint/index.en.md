Volley Club Nogentais is organizing its **Toussaint holiday tournament** and invites you to join for an evening that is
100 % volleyball, conviviality and competition!

<!--more-->

### 📅 Practical information

- **Date**: Tuesday 21 October 2025
- **Location**: Gymnasium Watteau, 37 rue Lequesne, 94130 Nogent‑Sur‑Marne
- **Schedule**:
  - **Arrival at 20:00** (welcome and warm‑up)
  - **Matches start at 20:30**
  - **Tournament ends at 22:30**

### 🏆 Tournament organization

- **9 teams of 6 players**: teams will be formed **randomly** to ensure balanced and fun match‑ups.
- **Format**: short‑format games to maximise playing time and enjoyment.
- **Level**: open to all, beginners as well as experienced players!

### 🍕 What to bring

- **Food and drinks**: each participant is invited to bring **something to eat and drink** to share a friendly moment
  after the matches.
- **Sportswear**: wear appropriate clothing and your best volleyball shoes.

### 📢 Why participate?

- To **have fun** and meet other volleyball enthusiasts.
- To **challenge** opponents in a relaxed atmosphere.
- To **celebrate** Toussaint in a sporty mode!

### 📩 How to register?

Registrations are **online only** via the
[dedicated form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeQcCYejsP4ysITeiy9R4Q9-t18mmRvJNP1jxOlQvwApvN8MA/viewform).
**Limited spots** – register quickly!

### ⚠️ Important

- **Mandatory presence at 20:00** for team composition.
- **No volleyball without good mood!** Fair‑play and team spirit are required.

### 💬 Questions?

Contact us via the community WhatsApp channels or through our social networks.

**See you soon on the courts for a memorable tournament!** 🎃🏐
