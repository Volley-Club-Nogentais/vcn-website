The **beach volleyball** section allows you to practice **2v2 beach volleyball on the banks of the Marne**, on two
dedicated courts, easily accessible from Paris and the whole Île‑de‑France region.

The section welcomes players who want to train, play freely, or take part in sporting and friendly events throughout the
year.

## Beach volleyball courts

- Two sand courts located on the banks of the Marne
- Pleasant site accessible from all of Île‑de‑France
- About a 10‑minute walk from the Nogent‑sur‑Marne RER A station

**Court address:**
[_Stade sous la Lune Alain Mimoun, 94170 Nogent-sur-Marne_](https://maps.app.goo.gl/85tF1r3ux4irL6Q8A)

## Beach volleyball trainings

The section offers sessions led by qualified coaches. These trainings help participants improve technically and
physically in a sporty and friendly atmosphere.

### Boys – advanced level

## Open play – court access

The club also provides **free access to the beach volleyball courts**.

Members can come and play **freely in the evenings and on weekends**, except when the courts are occupied by trainings:

❌ No access on Monday evenings and Wednesday evenings (training slots)

### How it works

- Free access to the courts
- No reservation required
- Unsupervised play
- Unlimited access to available slots
- Fee: **€60** (starting April)

### Reception hours

Reception will be available **some Saturdays from 14:00 to 18:00** to:

- inform interested people
- present the section
- enable registrations.

Dates are announced on the club’s social media.

## 🏆 Beach volleyball tournaments

## Raccoon Tour

The **Raccoon Tour** is the **friendly tournament of the VCN beach volleyball section**, organized **on a Friday evening
on two courts**. It is a flagship event of club life, allowing players to meet on the sand in a sporty, relaxed
atmosphere with an aperitif.

### Tournament format

🕕 Times: **18:45 – 21:30**

Teams **male, female or mixed**

Open to club members and outsiders.

### Fees

- Free for club members
- **€10 per team for outsiders**

After the tournament, participants can extend the evening with a **friendly aperitif**, a popular gathering for players.

➡️ Register via
[HelloAsso](https://www.helloasso.com/associations/volley-club-nogentais/adhesions/tournoi-raccoon-tour-du-vendredi-13-03)

## FBVS Series

The club organizes official Women’s, Men’s and Mixed tournaments of the France Beach Volley Series.

Dates and registrations are on the
[official site](http://bvs.ffvbbeach.org/mixte/page.php?P=fo/public/menu/tournoi/index).
