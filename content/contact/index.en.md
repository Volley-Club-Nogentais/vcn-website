Do you want to **join us**, **get information**, or **participate in our events**? Feel free to contact us!

### 📍 Where to find us?

Depending on the section, you can find us at the various gyms at the listed times.

### 📧 Send us a message

For any question, registration request, or partnership, you can write to us at: 📩
**[contact@volley-club-nogent.fr](mailto:contact@volley-club-nogent.fr)**

We commit to replying **as quickly as possible**.

### 📱 Follow us on social media

Stay informed about our news, results, and events by following us on:

- **[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/VolleyClubNogent/)**
- **[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/volleyclubnogent/)**
- **[Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@volleyclubnogent)**

### 🗣️ Prefer to talk to us live?

Find us **during training hours** or at our **events** (tournaments, matches, etc.). We’ll be happy to chat with you!

### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I come try out before committing?** Of course! We offer **two free trial sessions** to discover the club.

**Are there slots for beginners?** Yes, we welcome **all levels**, from beginners to advanced players.
