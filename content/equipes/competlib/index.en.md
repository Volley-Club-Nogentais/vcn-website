The **Canards Boiteux** (Lame Ducks) team is an iconic men’s 4x4 volleyball side, mainly made up of former departmental
players now in their forties. These players, drawing on their experience and passion for the sport, have reconvened on
the courts to continue sharing their love of volleyball while fostering a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. Despite the
years, their commitment and competitive spirit remain strong, making the Lame Ducks both formidable and endearing.

What distinguishes the Lame Ducks is their ability to combine **experience and good humour**. Having spent years at the
departmental level, they know the ins and outs of volleyball and can leverage their know‑how to offset the physical
limitations that come with age. Their game relies on **collective strategy**, fluid communication and mutual
understanding that only time can teach.

Each match is an opportunity to demonstrate that passion and game intelligence can rival youth and athleticism.

Beyond performance, the Lame Ducks embody a **philosophy of fun and camaraderie**. For them, volleyball is first and
foremost a way to gather with friends, share genuine laughter and keep alive a sporting tradition that has united them
for years. Their name, both humorous and evocative, perfectly reflects their spirit: a team that doesn’t take itself too
seriously, yet plays with heart and determination.

Whether on the court or off, the Lame Ducks create a warm, inclusive atmosphere that invites sympathy and respect.
