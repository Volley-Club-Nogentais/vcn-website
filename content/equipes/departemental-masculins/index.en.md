This season saw the birth of an entirely new group, built around a coach who returned to the club. With him, a nice
balance was created between promising youngsters and more experienced players, quickly forming a close‑knit and
ambitious team.

After a demanding selection at the start of the season, the goals were clear: promote the team from departmental to
regional level, so the men's section would have a true flagship team at this tier. Mission accomplished, with a
remarkable season, almost unbeaten, only marred by a few hiccups at the very end.

If the results speak for themselves, it is also thanks to the seriousness and dedication of the group: a team that
trained rigorously, made great progress and stayed united throughout the regular season.

The locker‑room atmosphere and each member’s investment were real strengths.

The story does not end there. With a few arrivals and departures, the group moves forward with ambition: riding this
great momentum and aiming, why not, for a second consecutive promotion. More than a team, it is a collective that lives
well and advances together, driven by the same passion and objectives.
