## Group Overview

The volleyball school is made up of several level groups to exploit each child's maximum potential and allow them to
develop within a close‑knit group of peers of the same age. Groups can change from one Saturday to the next depending on
each child's progress. All our youngsters train both to improve and to have fun, aiming for a step‑up to the under‑13
team, fueled by collective enthusiasm and energy. Our club’s goal: introduce the beauty of sport while building solid
fundamentals, and of course, have fun! We also guide them into matches so they experience all facets of volleyball.

Our values: we train our youth in an environment of respect, discipline and team spirit. We want them to play sport
while respecting teammates, opponents, referees and coaches. We push them to constantly improve while keeping enjoyment,
forming a strong, cohesive team that will evolve over the years with current and future teammates.

## Our Coaches

Our coaches are qualified and competent. We believe each coach brings experience and technical skills to the youngsters,
while teaching teamwork. That is why each coach mentors each group throughout the year.

## Our Achievements

In recent years, the volleyball school has been an excellent training ground for future under‑13 and under‑18 players,
who have impressed us with their work and performances. And that’s not all! The youths have also stood out in their
annual competitions, reaching regional competitions for the boys in 2025 and the final stage of the departmental cup for
the girls!!
