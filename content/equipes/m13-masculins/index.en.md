This season was a discovery year for this very young group, largely made up of beginners, with a few players who have a
bit more experience. The goal was clear: learn the fundamentals of volleyball, understand the principles of the game,
correct certain bad habits and, above all, have fun on the court.

As the matches progressed, the boys did far more than just have fun: they managed to upset every team they faced. The
only difficulty was a lack of consistency in performances, which prevented us from taking leading roles. We ultimately
finished in the lower half of the championship, but for a team still building and learning, this is a very nice
achievement.

It should also be noted that, in most matches, we faced opponents with a higher average age, yet this did not stop our
young players from fighting and showing a great mindset. This season laid solid foundations and hints at promising
prospects for the future.
