### 🏆 The Team 4x4 Saga: From Easy to Elite!

At **Volley Club Nogentais**, an extraordinary team has made championship 4x4 history. Formed **after COVID**, this
squad rose **from the Easy pool to the Elite**, combining **performance, perseverance, and good humor**.

#### 📈 A Rapid Rise

Created in the post‑pandemic era, the team began its adventure **at the lowest tier** of the 4x4 championship. Season
after season, it climbed the ranks:

- **Started in the Easy pool**: modest beginnings, but unwavering determination.
- **Progressive promotion**: thanks to a **strong team spirit** and **hard work**, victories and promotions followed.
- **Reached Elite**: today, it competes among the championship’s top teams!

The goal? **Stay in the Elite as long as possible** while continuing to improve.

#### 🤝 A Unique Atmosphere

What makes this team strong is far more than its playing level:

- **Unbreakable cohesion**: players get along perfectly, on and off the court.
- **Social moments**: after each match, **drinks** are shared to celebrate victories… or console defeats!
- **Winning mindset**: serious during matches, but always in **good humor** and **respect**.

#### 🎯 The Secret of Their Success?

- **Commitment**: every player gives their best in training and competition.
- **Consistency**: regular attendance and a **desire to always improve**.
- **Fun**: because volleyball, above all, is a **sport that must stay fun**!

#### 💬 Join the Adventure!

Do you also want to experience a **unique sporting and human** adventure? The team is always open to **motivated new
talent**.

**Contact us** to learn more or come cheer them on at upcoming matches!

**🏐 Together, let’s continue writing this beautiful story!**
